Campus life here at Uniford

Mission

Uniford is dedicated to fostering a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become innovative, and compassionate leaders. Through rigorous academics, hands-on learning, and a commitment to diversity and inclusion, we strive to cultivate critical thinkers who will contribute meaningfully to their communities and the global society. Our university embraces the pursuit of knowledge, and personal growth, preparing students for success in an ever-evolving world.

Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized institution that shapes the future through transformative education and a commitment to social responsibility. We aim to cultivate a dynamic learning environment that inspires creativity, and prepares students to address the world’s most pressing challenges. By nurturing leaders of tomorrow who are intellectually curious and socially conscious, we aspire to create a positive impact on society and contribute to the advancement of knowledge and culture.

Values

At Uniford, we are committed to fostering excellence in education, research, social improvement and community engagement. We uphold integrity, ensuring ethical standards in all academic and personal endeavors. Our diverse and inclusive environment values individuals of all backgrounds and perspectives, while promoting collaboration to achieve common goals. Innovation drives our approach, encouraging creativity and forward-thinking in solving various society and global challenges.

Uniford's History

Founded in 1988, Uniford has a rich history of academic excellence, innovation, and community impact. What began as a small institution dedicated to give students knowledge has grown into one of the leading universities in Africa, renowned for its diverse academic programs and commitment to shaping future leaders.
Over the years, the university has expanded its campus, introduced cutting-edge research initiatives, and developed strong connections with industries and global academic communities. Our legacy is built on a foundation of rigorous scholarship, a passion for discovery, and a dedication to the holistic development of students.
From its early years as a small tertiary school setup to becoming a modern hub for learning and innovation, Uniford continues to honor its founding principles while adapting to the evolving needs of the global educational landscape.
Today, we are proud of our history and the talented graduates who carry forward the university’s mission of excellence in their respective fields, contributing meaningfully to society and the world.

Uniford fields a number of competitive sports teams that regularly participate in regional and national tournaments. These teams include football, basketball, soccer, tennis, etc. Each team is coached by experienced professionals who guide students in honing their skills, building team spirit, and achieving excellence in their respective sports. We encourage student-athletes to push their limits, while also balancing academics and personal development.
For students who prefer a more relaxed approach to sports, we offer recreational leagues and intramural programs in various sports. Whether it’s a friendly game of badminton, tennis, or a co-ed soccer match, these activities provide a great way to stay active and socialize. Intramural sports allow students of all skill levels to compete in a less intense environment, promoting fitness and camaraderie among peers. Throughout the academic year, we organize a number of health and wellness events to promote a balanced lifestyle. These events include fitness challenges, sports tournaments, and workshops on mental well-being.
