Renovations on student dorms and cafeteria, solely funded by donations from sponsoring companies.

Our university is excited to unveil the newly renovated student dormitories and cafeteria, made possible through the generous donations from sponsoring companies. These upgrades represent a significant investment in the well-being and comfort of our students, providing them with modern, comfortable living and dining spaces.

The dormitories have been redesigned with improved facilities, including updated furniture, enhanced study areas, and more spacious rooms to foster a supportive living environment. The cafeteria has also undergone a complete transformation, offering students a wider variety of healthy, delicious meal options in a vibrant and inviting atmosphere.
