Ranked top 8th university in Africa

Uniford has proudly secured its place as one of the top eight universities in Africa, an achievement that speaks volumes about its commitment to excellence, innovation, and leadership in higher education. This prestigious ranking highlights the university’s remarkable contributions to research, academic quality, and the development of future leaders who are making an impact globally.
The recognition comes as a result of the university's exceptional academic programs, distinguished faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and groundbreaking research initiatives that address critical challenges in Africa and beyond. The university is renowned for its focus on providing a world-class education that blends academic rigor with real-world applications, preparing students to excel in a rapidly changing global landscape.
