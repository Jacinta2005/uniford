Interview with the recent appointed Student Affairs Administor; Mr Jacob Winthrob in his designated office.

In an exclusive interview, we had the opportunity to sit down with Mr. Jacob Wintrob, the recently appointed Student Affairs Administrator at our university. In his newly designated office, Mr. Wintrob shared his vision and goals for enhancing the student experience on campus. With a deep commitment to student well-being and success, he discussed his plans to foster a supportive, inclusive, and dynamic environment for all students.

Mr. Wintrob emphasized the importance of open communication, providing students with resources to thrive academically, socially, and personally. He also spoke about his focus on developing new initiatives that promote mental health awareness, career development, and student engagement, ensuring that every student feels valued and heard.
